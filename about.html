Our Story

🕮 Where We Began

Our story begins in 1995 on the tropical Hawaiian island of Kauaʻi, where founders John Berry and brothers Carl and Brett Harwin ran a small, bottled-juice stand out of tents set in a papaya field. Captivated by the island's focus on pure, natural foods and an active lifestyle, they recognized a massive opportunity to bring this clean, wholesome approach back to South Africa.

In 1996, the first Kauai store officially opened its doors in Cape Town, pioneering a healthy fast-casual revolution. We introduced South Africans to their very first real fruit smoothies, freshly rolled wraps, and crisp salads made to order. Kauai quickly became a beloved staple for health-conscious communities, athletes, and fitness enthusiasts.

A milestone came in 2015 when Kauai was acquired by the multi-brand food group Real Foods. Under this partnership, we modernized our stores, expanded our reach, and committed to a strict standard: using 100% natural, GMO-free ingredients with no artificial colorants, flavorings, or preservatives.

Today, Kauai has expanded to over 170 locations across South Africa, including our signature outlets inside Virgin Active health clubs, standalone community hubs, and premium shopping centres—all continuing the legacy of making healthy eating delicious, accessible, and convenient.
